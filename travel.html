Traditional Foods ...

Iceland is widely known for its cold temperatures but despite this fact one of the most common dessert is cream cheese. This dessert consist of an ice cream that is flavored by a sweet cheese. This desert is commonly ate with bits of cheese crust crumbled at the top.
